About Our Club

Based in Frontier, MI, the Hillsdale County On Target 4-H Club is a welcoming community for youth ages 5–19. We focus on creativity, learning, growth, and fun through both livestock and non-livestock projects. Members explore Art, Public Speaking, Creative Projects, Writing, Pottery, Painting, and more—all while building confidence, leadership, and lifelong skills.

Projects We Offer

Art

Explore drawing, painting, mixed media, and creative expression.

Public Speaking

Build confidence through speeches, presentations, and demonstrations.

Creative Projects

Hands-on crafts, design challenges, and imaginative builds.

Writing

Develop storytelling, poetry, essays, and creative writing skills.

Pottery

Learn hand-building, sculpting, and glazing techniques.

Painting

Experiment with color, style, and technique across mediums.
