Our Members

Growing, learning, and creating together as a 4‑H family

Who We Are

The Hillsdale County On Target 4‑H Club is home to youth ages 5–19 who love to learn, create, explore new skills, and support each other. Our members come from all over Frontier and the surrounding area, bringing their unique talents and interests to the club.

Member Groups

Cloverbuds (Ages 5–7)

Our youngest members explore creativity, teamwork, and hands‑on fun in a safe, supportive environment.

Juniors (Ages 8–12)

Juniors dive deeper into projects, build confidence, and discover new interests through guided learning.

Teens (Ages 13–19)

Teen members take on leadership roles, mentor younger youth, and develop advanced project skills.

What Members Do

Members participate in hands‑on projects, workshops, community service, public speaking, art, creative projects, and more. Whether they’re learning pottery techniques, preparing a presentation, or helping at a club event, every member grows in confidence and skill.
