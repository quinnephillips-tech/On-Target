Our Project Areas

Art

Members explore drawing, painting, mixed media, and creative expression while learning technique and style.

Public Speaking

Youth build confidence through speeches, demonstrations, presentations, and communication workshops.

Creative Projects

Hands‑on crafts, design challenges, and imaginative builds that encourage problem‑solving and innovation.

Writing

Members develop storytelling, poetry, essays, and creative writing skills through guided activities.

Pottery

Youth learn hand‑building, sculpting, glazing, and the fundamentals of working with clay.

Painting

Members experiment with color, technique, and style across a variety of painting mediums.

Livestock (Optional)

For members interested in animals, we offer opportunities to learn about care, responsibility, and showmanship.
